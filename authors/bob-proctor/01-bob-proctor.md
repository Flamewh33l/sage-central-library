# Bob Proctor

## Overview
Bob Proctor (1934-2022) was a Canadian self-help author and motivational speaker, best known for his work on the law of attraction, paradigm shifting, and success mindset. He was a key figure in the personal development industry for over 50 years.

## Major Works

### "You Were Born Rich" (1984)
- His most famous work
- Focuses on unlocking the mental potential people are born with
- Key concepts: paradigm shifts, the subconscious mind, wealth consciousness

### "The Proctor Gallagher Institute" (founded with Sandy Gallagher)
- Educational platform for personal development
- Courses on mindset, success, and abundance

## Key Concepts

### Paradigms
- Mental programs that control our behavior
- Shifting paradigms = shifting results
- Most people operate on paradigms inherited from family/society, not chosen consciously

### The Mind
- Conscious mind: thinking, choosing (5% of mental activity)
- Subconscious mind: conditioned responses, habits (95% of mental activity)
- Changing the subconscious = changing automatic behavior

### The Law of Attraction
- Thoughts become things
- Vibrational frequency attracts similar frequencies
- Featured prominently in "The Secret" (2006)

## Impact on Cognitive Development

1. **Awareness of mental programming** - Understanding that our thoughts aren't always "ours" but inherited patterns
2. **Intentional thinking** - Choosing thoughts deliberately rather than operating on autopilot
3. **Persistence and repetition** - Changing deep patterns requires consistent effort
4. **Goal visualization** - The practice of mentally rehearsing desired outcomes
5. **Responsibility for mindset** - Owning your mental state rather than blaming circumstances

## Criticisms to Consider
- Pseudoscientific claims about "vibration" and "frequency"
- Overemphasis on positive thinking without addressing structural barriers
- Commercialization of self-help industry
